Who Are We?

Panda

Panda has a lot less self-confidence than his brothers. He is good with technology, and uses that skill on online social media and dating sites, with which he finds little to no success. He is also the one who "falls in love" the most quickly.

Ice Bear

Ice Bear is often stoic and rarely speaks. When he does, he speaks mostly in the third person and mostly says his name first before anything else. He is the most composed of the brothers, always maintaining an "ice-cool attitude" in even the most awkward and embarrassing of situations.

Grizzly

The oldest and the de facto leader of the brothers, Grizz is a fun-loving bear who tends to get the others into random hijinks. Grizz is paradoxically highly sociable yet socially inept. Despite this, he is constantly wanting to meet new people and tries to befriend everyone he comes across.
